## 🟡 TIER 3: REGIME RULES
*Framework for what strategies to run in what market conditions*

---

### RULE 16 — BEAR/STRONG BEAR = ALT OUTPERFORMANCE REGIME (COUNTERINTUITIVE)

| Regime | Days | Avg Alt Daily Return | Positive Days |
|--------|------|---------------------|---------------|
| STRONG_BULL | 737 | +0.87% | 54% |
| BULL | 504 | +1.95% | 50% |
| BEAR | 565 | +2.45% | 46% |
| STRONG_BEAR | 458 | **+7.87%** | 47% |

**Key insight:** Alts have **higher average daily returns in bear markets**, but with lower positive-day frequency. This means: in bear regimes, returns are driven by **violent, infrequent bounces** — not steady uptrends.

**Regime rules:**
- **STRONG_BULL:** Deploy RSI strategies, expect slow grind. Consistent small wins. Hold longer.
- **BULL:** Best overall environment. All strategies valid. Standard position sizes.
- **BEAR:** Oversold bounces are real and profitable. But 54% of days go down. **Reduce hold times. Take profits faster. Do not hodl alts.**
- **STRONG_BEAR:** The +7.87% average is dominated by extreme outlier bounces. **Only trade RSI oversold signals. Never hold positions overnight without a stop. The 47% positive day rate means most days you lose — wait for your setup.**

---

### RULE 17 — HOW TO IDENTIFY CURRENT REGIME
*(Working definition — apply to BTC price action)*

| Regime | BTC Condition |
|--------|--------------|
| STRONG_BULL | BTC >50-day MA, >200-day MA, making higher highs |
| BULL | BTC above 200-day MA, some pullbacks |
| BEAR | BTC below 200-day MA, rallies fail |

**Adjust position sizes by regime:**
- STRONG_BULL: 1x
- BULL: 1.25x (best overall conditions)
- BEAR: 0.75x (higher volatility, lower win % on daily basis)
- STRONG_BEAR: 0.5x base, but 1.5x on confirmed RSI signals only

## 🚨 TRAPS TO AVOID

---

### TRAP 1 — CHASING BTC AFTER +10% PUMP ❌
- BTC after +10% pump: **dumps 65% of time**, avg 3d return **-1.5%** (20 events)
- **RULE: Never long BTC after a +10% single-day move. If you missed it, you missed it.**
- This is one of the clearest negative edges in the dataset.

---

### TRAP 2 — CHASING XRP AFTER +20% PUMP ❌
- XRP after +20% pump: dumps 67% of time, avg 3d return only **+0.1%** (18 events)
- XRP after +10% pump: dumps 57%, avg +0.8% (68 events)
- **RULE: XRP pumps are traps. The only XRP edge is buying RSI < 15, not chasing momentum.**

---

### TRAP 3 — CHASING LINK AFTER ANY PUMP ❌
- LINK +10% pump: 52% dump, avg **-2.2%** (54 events)
- LINK +20% pump: 60% dump, avg **-3.7%** (5 events)
- **RULE: LINK is the worst pump-chasing coin in the dataset. It has a RSI<20 long edge but zero pump momentum edge. Never chase LINK.**

---

### TRAP 4 — CHASING ADA AFTER +20% PUMP ❌
- ADA +20% pump: dumps 58%, avg **-2.8%** (12 events)
- Despite ADA having massive historical single-day moves (+72%), chasing these is losing.
- **RULE: ADA mega-pumps are not chaseable. They are parabolic events that reverse. Only trade RSI < 30 entries.**

---

### TRAP 5 — USING VOLUME SPIKES FOR DIRECTION ❌
- ALL coins: 3x volume spike direction continuation = **42–56% across all assets** (100+ events each)
- This is essentially a coin flip with slight noise.
- **RULE: Volume spikes alone tell you nothing reliable about direction. Use volume as a FILTER (confirms other signals) not as a primary signal.**

---

### TRAP 6 — USING BTC LEADER-LAGGER AS PREDICTOR ❌
- BTC move → next candle continuation: **44–47% across all alts**
- **RULE: Do not assume alts will continue BTC's move on the next candle. Beta is real in the same candle, but the next candle is a near-coin-flip.**

---

### TRAP 7 — SHORTING DOGE AFTER PUMPS ❌
- DOGE after +10% pump: only dumps 57%, but avg 3d return is **+7.4%** (97 events)
- DOGE after +20% pump: dumps only 55%, avg 3d return **+18.7%** (31 events)
- **RULE: DOGE is the ONE coin where momentum continuation after pumps is real. Do not short DOGE pumps. The avg return is too high and the dump frequency is not high enough to justify shorts.**

---

### TRAP 8 — TREATING >5% BTC DROP AS A RELIABLE LONG ❌
- BTC after >5% drop: only 51% bounce (101 events), avg 1d only +0.8%
- **RULE: Small crashes in BTC are NOT reliably tradeable. Wait for >10% drops (77% WR) or combine with RSI < 20.**

---

### TRAP 9 — ASSUMING RSI OVERSOLD PREVENTS FURTHER CRASHES ❌
- BTC crashed -15.7% with RSI already at 15 (Aug 2024)
- AVAX crashed -27.1% with RSI at 30, then -22.6% with RSI at 13
- BNB crashed -17.8% with RSI at 11
- **RULE: RSI oversold predicts the RECOVERY, not the BOTTOM. Always enter in tranches. Always use a hard stop. Never go all-in on RSI signals.**
